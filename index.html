REST IN PEACE; IBRAHIM RAOSO

Ebrahim Raisi was an influential Iranian cleric, prosecutor, and politician. He served as the head of the judiciary from 2019 to 2021, and as Iran's president from 2021 until his death in 2024. Born on December 14, 1960, in Mashhad, Iran, he was a key figure in Iran's political landscape. Raisi tragically died in a helicopter crash on May 19, 2024, in East Azerbaijan, Iran. His death was a significant event, marking the end of his tenure and impacting Iran's political dynamics.
